Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna Recipe (with Video)

Indulge in the comforting layers of rich, savory flavors with this classic lasagna recipe. Perfect for gatherings or cozy family dinners, this dish combines tender lasagna noodles, hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and a blend of melted mozzarella and Parmesan. Each bite is a symphony of textures and tastes, from the robustness of the seasoned meat sauce to the creamy richness of the ricotta filling. Baked to golden perfection, this lasagna is not only a delight to the taste buds but also a warm embrace of home-cooked goodness. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe promises to deliver a satisfying meal that will have everyone asking for seconds.

Ingredients:

    -
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • - 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • - 1 onion, diced
  • - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • - 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • - 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • - Salt and pepper to taste
  • - 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • - 1 egg
  • - 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until they're al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and chopped parsley until well combined.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place three lasagna noodles on top. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of the meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
